*Ingredients:*

1. Cucumber
1. Tomatoes
1. Bell pepper
1. Kalamata olives
1. Olive oil
1. Balsamic vinegar
1. Lemon juice
1. Feta cheese
1. Dill
1. Mint
1. [Roasted chickpeas](/recipes/roasted-chickpeas/)

*Directions:*

1. Chop everything into roughly even pieces.
1. Mix the chopped pieces together.
1. Dress with olive oil, balsamic, lemon juice, feta, and herbs to taste.
